Family Programs

Our family programs are designed to engage visitors of all ages in the world of scrimshaw. From hands-on workshops to interactive exhibits, we offer a variety of activities that allow gueststo explore the art and history of scrimshaw together.

Lecture Series

Join us for a series of lectures on the history and techniques of scrimshaw. Our staff of experts will share insights into the art form of Scrimshaw and its cultural significance.
