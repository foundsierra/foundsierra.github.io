Family Programs

Our family programs are designed to engage visitors of all ages in the world of scrimshaw. From hands-on workshops to interactive exhibits, we offer a variety of activities that allow families to explore the art and history of scrimshaw together.

Lecture Series

Join us for a series of informative lectures on the history and techniques of scrimshaw. Our experts will share insights into the art form and its cultural significance.
