Modern Razor

Materials: Metal, Antler

Artist: Robert Monfils Jr.

Date: 1980s

Born in New Bedford, Massachusetts, Robert Monfils learned the art of scrimshaw from his grandfather. His 30 year career as a scrimshander began when he moved to the Northwest in 1976. Monfils’ work captured intricate wildlife and nautical scenes. This piece combines the raw materials found in the wilderness with the picturesque views of lighthouses off the coastlines.
