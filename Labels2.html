Straight Razor

Materials: Metal, Plastic

This double sided razor depicts a woman with the banner, “Liberty” surrounding her as she adorns a gown and feathered head piece. On the reverse side, her gown is now removed leaving her nude except for her head piece and boots. A look of surprise is carved into her face as the banner surrounding her now states, “Freedom.”

Q: How would a female scrimshader depict themes of freedom and liberty in their work?
