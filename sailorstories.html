Scrimshaw Museum

Sailor Stories

Join us every first Saturday of the month for our Sailor Stories event, hosted by local comedian and drag queen, Jilly Roger. Join Jilly as she shares comedic stories inspired by history on lives of sailors, scrimshanders and whalers. Each month will feature a different theme and different guest performers and speakers. Some of our events are not suited for children, so please check the event description before purchasing tickets. All proceeds from the event will go towards the museum's educational and outreachprograms. We hope to see you there!

Upcoming Events

  • Saturday, July 6th: "Tales of the Sea"
    • Jilly hosts an evening of storytelling and comedy as shares classic seafaring tales and dramatic whaling mishaps. Listen in to our head curator, Sierra, talk about the importance of scrimshaw as medium of documentation. This event is suitable for children.
  • August 3rd: "Sailor's Love Stories"
    • Join us for an evening of romance and comedy as Jilly Roger shares tales of sailor's love stories, inspired by the lives of sailors, scrimshanders and whalers. Take this opportunity to view the museum's collection of scrimshaw depicting erotism and lust. This event is not suitable for children.
  • September 7th: "Sailor Shanties"
    • Join us for an evening of music and comedy as Jilly Roger teaches classic sea shanties and performs a spellbounding performance along with local band, Biniki Bottoms. This event is suitable for all ages.
    • Hours: Monday - Saturday: 10am - 5pm, Sunday: 12pm - 5pm. The Scrimshaw Museum is closed on major holidays.
